Privacy Policy

Code Pyjamas (“We”) are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors to our web site. In this Privacy Policy, we’ve provided details which information we collect or that you provide to us and how we use it (your “information”).

Types of information we collect

Information you give us.

We collect information that you give us when you sign up to use CodePyjamas.com (the “Site”) or contact us by email, phone or otherwise. The information is given to us when you show an interest using our Site to browse jobs, signup, post a job, apply for a job, subscribe to a newsletter. The information may include your name, e-mail address, your company details, job post details.

Information automatically collected

When you visit our Site, we use a third party service, Google Analytics, to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behaviour patterns. It will collect the information including the full Uniform Resource Locators (URL), clickstream to, through and from our Site (including date and time), page response times, length of visits to certain pages, browser type, operating system and platform. This information is only processed in a way which does not identify anyone. We do not make or allow Google to make any attempt to find out the identities of those visiting our site.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our site and allow you to access certain features.

How do we use your information?

We use the information you give us to provide the service that you request from us and to notify you of any changes to our service.

We use the information that is automatically collected to improve our services. This includes ensuring that content is presented in the most effective way for your and your computer, monitoring any errors, troubleshooting, data analysis and survey purposes.

Access to your personal information

If you wish to access, change or delete your information collected by us, you can contact us at team (at) codepyjamas.com

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We keep our Privacy Policy under regular review. If there are any changes to our Privacy Policy in the future, we will post it on this page.
